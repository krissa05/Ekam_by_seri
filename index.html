Our Roots

The Living Art of
Kutch

In the vast salt flats and ochre deserts of Kutch, Gujarat, an extraordinary tradition of artistry has flourished for centuries. The women of Kutch perfected the art of mud relief — pressing geometric patterns into clay walls, embedding mirror fragments to capture the desert light, weaving stories of devotion and daily life into every surface they touched.

Ekam by Seri was born from a deep reverence for this living heritage — a belief that these handcrafted traditions deserve not merely preservation, but a place of honour in contemporary homes. Every piece we create is a conversation between the ancient and the present.
