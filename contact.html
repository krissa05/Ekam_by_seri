Questions

Common questions

Do you take custom or bulk orders? +

Yes. We take custom sizes, custom motifs, and bulk orders for gifting or events. Mention your requirements in your message and we will discuss timeline and pricing with you directly.

How long does it take to receive my order? +

Every piece is made to order. Standard lead time is 2–3 weeks from confirmation. Larger or custom pieces may take slightly longer — we will always confirm before you commit.

Do you ship outside India? +

Currently we ship across India. For international enquiries, please reach us on WhatsApp and we will do our best to arrange shipping to your location.

Can I see a piece before committing? +

We can send photos and a short video of similar finished pieces so you have a clear sense of what to expect. We also send a photo of your specific piece before it ships.

What is your return or exchange policy? +

Because each piece is made to order, we are unable to accept returns for change of mind. If a piece arrives damaged in transit, please photograph it immediately and contact us — we will resolve it.
