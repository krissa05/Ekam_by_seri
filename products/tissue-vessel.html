The Story

The luxury of the daily object

A keepsake box is where a home stores its small treasures. Your Tissue Vessel is built on a sturdy base, then surfaced by hand in mud relief and mirror so the container is as considered as what it holds.

Hinges, lids and trays are fitted by hand and checked individually. The Tissue Vessel is made to be opened and closed for years, not admired once and shelved.

Once you message us, an artisan begins your piece by hand. Because the work is done individually, your Tissue Vessel takes about two to three weeks before it ships.
